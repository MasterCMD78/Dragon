The frontend shows:

READY TO CLAIM!

However:

* no Claim button is rendered
* tapping READY TO CLAIM does absolutely nothing

Expected behaviour:

When claimable == false

* show countdown
* hide Claim button

When claimable == true

* show READY TO CLAIM
* render Claim button
* Claim button must call the mining claim endpoint
* after successful claim:
    * update HP
    * refresh mining history
    * immediately start the next mining session (if that is the intended design)

Audit the mining React component only.

Do not assume the backend is wrong until you verify the frontend rendering.
